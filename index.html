PURCHASE PLATE
If you have not purchased a pre-sale voucher, you may purchase a plate here. Select Special Interest plates, then scroll down to Florida Stands with Israel, and click Purchase to complete your order.

How to purchase a plate

Step 1: Click “Purchase Online” button below.
Purchase Online
NOTE: You will be directed to the Escambia County Tax Collector ("ECTC") website. Residents of ALL Florida counties may purchase the license plate through the ECTC website.
Step 2: Under “Specialty License Plates”, click the “Special Interests” dropdown menu.
Step 3: Scroll down to the “Florida Stands With Israel” plate and click the “Purchase” button below.
Step 4: Enter your license plate number and click the “Pay Now” button.
Step 5: Enter payment information and click “Review Payment”.
Step 6: Follow instructions to submit payment.
Step 7: Your license plate will be mailed to you within 1-3 weeks.
NOTE:
License plate fees may differ based on the status of your registration.

If you purchased a pre-sale voucher, please read the following information to get your plate. This ONLY applies to a small number of customers who pre-ordered the license plate in 2024 or earlier. Contact us below if you need assistance.
Purchase by visiting your local Tax Collector office

Locations can be found via FLHSMV by clicking the “Find Office” button below. Going in person is the quickest way to get your plate. Folks are usually able to leave with their plate the same day.

Every Plate Saves Lives

The $25 specialty license plate annual renewal fee will benefit Hatzalah South Florida Emergency Medical Services (HSF EMS) – a 24/7 emergency response organization run entirely by volunteers, offering critical care free of charge to all. HSF EMS responds to close to 10,000 calls in Florida every year with over 300 volunteers, 18 ambulances and 20 emergency rescue vehicles.

www.hsfems.org
Governor Ron DeSantis signed the specialty license plate into law, emphasizing its role in strengthening the long standing bond between Florida and Israel. "I am committed to deepening the relationship between Florida and the state of Israel," Governor DeSantis remarked. "This specialty license plate serves as a visible symbol of our steadfast support and friendship."

$25 of every $33 pre-sale voucher will benefit Hatzalah South Florida (HSF).
  • You can purchase multiple vouchers using the same Florida license plate number. If you're interested in purchasing 10 or more vouchers, contact: info@floridastandswithisrael.org
  • Non-Florida residents can purchase vouchers as gifts for Florida residents using the recipient's Florida license plate number and full name.
  • Purchasing a voucher supports the initiative but does not obligate you to redeem it for the specialty license plate.
